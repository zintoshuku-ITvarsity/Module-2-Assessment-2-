ABOUT PINE CITY THEATRE OF SHOPPING

Pine City Theatre of Shopping is the ultimate fashion, entertainment and dinning destination in Pine City. With over 420+ stores, 70 eateries and a host of exiciting entertainment including the Pine City Zoo behind us, visitors are spoilt for choice.

At our Mall you will find local South Affricans retails, popular international brands such as Zaea, Forever New, Cotton On, Grand, and everything in between. Our shooping center also features an 18 cinema Ster Kinekor Complex, IMAX Theatre, Electric Avenue bowling, Park, Family Funfair and so much more.

Prepare your senses for a world class experience at PINE CITY SHOPPING CENTRE.
