MUGG & BEAN

Mugg & Bean is a place where customers become regulars and regulars become friends. We are your home away from home, the place where you'll always get more and more. This is the essence of Mugg & Bean.Mugg & Bean is a full-service, & On-The-Move restaurant, coffee-themed franchise restaurant chain originating from South Africa. The restaurant chain was founded in 1996 by Ben Filmalter after a visit to a Chicago coffee shop in the early 1990s inspired him to open a similar restaurant in South Africa.
