SPITZ

Opened in 1968 by Anthony and David Spitz, Spitz started life as a single store in Johannesburg CBD. Now with over 70 stores country wide Spitz is synonymous with the exclusive international brands we offer in store and renowned as South Africa's premier footwear retailer.
